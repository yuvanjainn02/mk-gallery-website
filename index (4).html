Our Heritage

A Legacy of Fine Craftsmanship

For over four decades, M K Gallery has stood as an icon of luxury soft furnishings in Chickpet, Bengaluru. Established in 1981, we specialize in weaving comfort, heritage, and sophistication into every home and hotel space.

From custom-tailored drapes in heavy velvet and linen to highly resilient commercial hotel linens, our Bengaluru studio delivers hand-finished precision. We source, measure, stitch, and install with a standard of excellence that has dressed the city's finest interiors for 45 years.
